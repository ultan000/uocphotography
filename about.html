About Ultan

Ultan O'Cinnéide is a photographer based in Navan, Co. Meath. Specialising in both portrait and wedding photography, he provides high quality, professional pictures for any agenda.

Having originally picked up a camera to make his own music videos, it wasn't long before the lure of still photographs attracted Ultan. He made it his mission to learn any techniques he could, be it compositional or technical, and his skills rapidly progressed. Taking on any and all work that he could, it wasn't long before he knew that this passion was where his career path lied.
